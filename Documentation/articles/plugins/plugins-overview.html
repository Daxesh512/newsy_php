Plugins Overview

Newsy has different plugins and always tries to do its best not only in quality and features of plugins but also to provide a satisfying support of plugins. Most of the Newsy plugins are among the most strong ones of their field.

Newsy plugins are fully compatible with Newsy theme and most of the standard WordPress themes but for now they are exclusive for Newsy theme and cannot be downloaded or purchased (it might change in future).

Newsy plugins are included in Newsy package and add various and amazing features to that. Also there are other highly compatible free and premium plugins included in Newsy.

An Important Note About Premium Plugins Activation

We bought extended license of WPBakery Page Builder, so there is no need every individual user activate these two plugins by an activation code. Every time they get an update, you will see them in Newsy plugin update center (Go to Newsy → Install Plugins). Update them from here and don’t worry about their activation.

These type of plugins are called Bundled Plugins; they may ask for a purchase code for registration/activation once installed, however, you can simply ignore these messages as bundled plugins do not require activation or registration. The plugin will still work as intended with the theme once the Newsy has been registered.
